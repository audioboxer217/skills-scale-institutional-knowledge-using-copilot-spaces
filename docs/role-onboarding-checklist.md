# OctoAcme — Role Onboarding & Handoff Checklist

## Purpose
Use this checklist when a person **takes on a new role** (onboarding) or **transfers responsibilities** to someone else (handoff). Completing this checklist reduces single-person dependency and ensures continuity.

---

## Section 1: Access & Tools

- [ ] GitHub repository access granted (appropriate permission level)
- [ ] Project board access confirmed (GitHub Projects or equivalent)
- [ ] Communication channels joined (Slack, Teams, or equivalent — project channels and DMs)
- [ ] Document storage access verified (shared drive, Confluence, Notion, etc.)
- [ ] CI/CD pipeline access granted (if required for role)
- [ ] Monitoring and alerting dashboards accessible (if required for role)
- [ ] Calendar invites accepted for recurring ceremonies (standup, planning, retrospective, demos)

## Section 3: Stakeholder Introductions

- [ ] Met with Project Manager (PM)
- [ ] Met with Product Manager (PdM)
- [ ] Met with Scrum Master
- [ ] Met with QA Lead
- [ ] Met with key Developers on the team
- [ ] Introduced to primary Stakeholders
- [ ] Introduced to Sponsor (if role requires direct interaction)
- [ ] Introduced to UX Designer (if role requires design collaboration)

---

## Section 4: Current Sprint & Board Context

- [ ] Reviewed the current sprint goal and sprint backlog
- [ ] Confirmed understanding of items currently **In Progress** and their owners
- [ ] Reviewed items in **In Review** and **QA** columns — understand blockers or pending actions
- [ ] Identified any overdue items or items at risk of missing the sprint goal
- [ ] Confirmed which ceremonies you are expected to attend this sprint

---

## Section 5: Pending PRs & Code Context (Developers / QA Lead)

- [ ] Reviewed all open PRs — understand status and any blocking review comments
- [ ] Identified PRs pending QA sign-off or approval
- [ ] Reviewed recent merged PRs for context on current feature state
- [ ] Confirmed branch naming and PR conventions used by the team

---

## Section 6: Outstanding Risks & Action Items

- [ ] Reviewed the risk register (`docs/octoacme-risks-and-communication.md`)
- [ ] Identified top 3–5 active risks and their current mitigation status
- [ ] Confirmed who owns each active risk after the handoff
- [ ] Reviewed open retrospective action items — confirm ownership and due dates
- [ ] Identified any unresolved escalations or pending decisions

---

## Section 7: Knowledge Transfer Session

- [ ] Scheduled a live handoff / knowledge transfer session with the outgoing role holder (if applicable)
- [ ] Recorded the session or captured notes for async review
- [ ] Confirmed any informal processes or context not captured in documentation
- [ ] Outgoing role holder available for follow-up questions for at least [X] days after handoff
- [ ] PM and Scrum Master notified that the handoff is complete
